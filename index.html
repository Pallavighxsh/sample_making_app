Sample Copy Generator

An editorial utility for preparing controlled, share-ready textbook sample copies without altering their structure or intent.

  1. Upload a textbook PDF (up to 200 MB).
  2. Pages 1–3 remain unchanged.
  3. From page 4 onward, ~45% of pages are randomly blacked out.
  4. The file is processed securely in one pass.
  5. Download the processed PDF instantly.
